 USAGE:

   Converts a normalized threshold to waves per meter. Assumes isotropic
    in-plane voxels. Applies norm thresh to smallest dimension h or w.

 INPUTS:

   thresh - normalized threshold
    voxel_spacing - in meters

 OUTPUTS:

   none
